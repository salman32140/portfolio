Performance Management System

Working as the developer of the Performance Management System , Bulleh Shah Packaging Ltd. allowed me to work as the full stack developer responsible of Database Management, Front-End and Back-End Developer. PMS took 2 years to become a mature product and now it entertains more than 2000 employees of different grades from packages group companies to carry out their yearly performance evaluations and rate their direct appraises. Technologies used for this project are :

  1. MySql Database
  2. PHP (Core)
  3. Jquery

The primary aim of Performance management system is to establish a framework for enabling the organization to accelerate results by building a high performance culture and establishing a system where transparency is ensured and corporate goals are aligned with the business unit, department performance objectives and individual KPIs.Improved shareholder value, greater profitability, increase revenue, inspired innovation – none of these can be realized without an organized process that deconstructs the corporate goals and cascades relevant and measurable elements of them through the workforce.We firmly believe that our people should be recognized and held accountable for their performance, leading to ownership and responsibility for continuous improvement of business processes and self-development under effective leadership.

Performance Management System is now a part of three group companies and the potential of this projects allows it to be implemented in other group companies as well. Currently following Packages group companies are using this product.

  • Bulleh Shah Packaging Ltd.
  • Packages Ltd.
  • Packages Mall
  • Tripack
  • DIC Ltd.
